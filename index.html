Osamu Dazai

Contemporary Novelist, Short Story Writer

Born as Shuji Tsushima, Dazai was a Japanese author who is considered one of the foremost fiction writers of 20th-century Japan. A number of his most popular works, such as The Setting Sun and No Longer Human, are considered modern-day classics in Japan.

Notable Works by Dazai:

  • Omoide (Memories) - 1933
  • Doke no Hana (Flowers of Buffoonery) - 1935
  • Hashire Merosu (Run, Melos!) - 1940
  • Tsugaru (Tsugaru) - 1944
  • Shayo (The Setting Sun) - 1947
  • Ningen Shikkaku (No Longer Human) - 1948
  • Goddo-bai (Good-Bye) - 1948
